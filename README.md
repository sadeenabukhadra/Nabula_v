## Overview

AI-Enhanced Habitat Designer (Nebula-V) is an interactive Unity-based 3D experience that allows users to design, explore, and optimize space habitats in a fully immersive environment.

The project transforms complex space engineering concepts into an interactive simulation, making habitat planning visual, accessible, and engaging.

It combines gameplay mechanics with AI-driven decision support to simulate how humans might live and operate in future space missions.

---

## Core Experience

The user experience includes multiple interactive systems:

- First-person exploration inside modular space habitats
- Design and arrangement of functional rooms such as crew quarters, hygiene units, galley, exercise areas, and science laboratories
- AI Mission Advisor chatbot that guides design decisions and simulates mission-style communication
- AI Layout Evaluation system that analyzes habitat design based on constraints such as crew size, mission duration, and spatial efficiency
- Interactive simulation environments including recycling systems and a custom room creation mode for experimentation and creative design

## Why This Project Matters

This project makes space habitat design accessible beyond engineering experts.

It allows users to:
- Understand how astronauts might live in confined environments
- Experiment with realistic constraints such as space, resources, and crew needs
- Learn principles of sustainability and system design in space habitats
- Explore the intersection between artificial intelligence, architecture, and simulation

It turns space engineering into an interactive learning experience rather than a purely theoretical concept.

## Technology Stack

- Unity Engine for 3D environment development and simulation

---

## System Architecture (High-Level)

The project is structured into three main layers:

1. Interactive Simulation Layer  
   Handles player movement, exploration, and building interactions inside the Unity environment.

2. AI Assistance Layer  
   Provides conversational guidance through the Mission Advisor chatbot and evaluates habitat designs using predefined constraints and logic.

3. Data and Logic Layer  
   Manages room definitions, spatial constraints, mission parameters, and evaluation rules used by the AI systems.

---

## Vision

The project aims to bridge artificial intelligence, human creativity, and space science into a single interactive experience.

It enables users to learn through experimentation, design under constraints, and explore future living environments beyond Earth.

The long-term vision is to make complex scientific and engineering problems more understandable through simulation and interaction.
